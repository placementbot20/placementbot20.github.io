About Placement Bot

The Placement Bot is an AI powered chatbot that provides job specific, time bound and personalized guidance for placements including tips on competitive coding, aptitude, logical reasoning, interviews, online tests and career advice for on-campus and off-campus placements.

The motive behind this voluntary initiative was to help engineering students prepare for placements in a more personalised way and eliminate the one-size-fit-all guidance provided all over the web. If you are passionate about helping students and would volunteer to contribute content with us do send in a message!
